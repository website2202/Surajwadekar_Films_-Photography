About the Photographer

Meet Surajwadekar

Suraj wadekar is a passionate photographer with over 8 years of experience in capturing life's most beautiful moments. Specializing in wedding and portrait photography, Suraj's work is known for its creativity, precision, and the ability to tell a story through each photograph.

Suraj developed a love for photography at an early age. Over the years, he has honed his skills, traveling across the country and working with a diverse range of clients. His portfolio includes everything from grand weddings to intimate portraits, showcasing his versatility and talent.

When he's not behind the camera, Suraj enjoys exploring new places, meeting new people, and finding inspiration in the world around him. His goal is to not only capture moments but to create lasting memories that his clients can cherish forever.
